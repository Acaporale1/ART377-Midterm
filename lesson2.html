Now that we understand the HTML structure, let's dive into formatting text!



  • One way to show hierarchhy within your site is through the use of 'HEADINGS'.

  • There are 6 'levels' of HEADINGS.
    • A heading tag in HTML is represented as an 'h' followed by the heading level number, enclosed in angled brackets.
    • Any text placed between the open and closed heading tag will inherit the specified level's size properties. (This can be customized later on...)
